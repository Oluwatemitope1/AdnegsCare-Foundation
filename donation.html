Help people who are in need

It is not how much we give but how much love we put into giving.

Millions of people find it hard to cater for themselves

“There is no better exercise for your heart than reaching down and helping to lift someone up” - Bernard Meltzer. Join us today in helping to lift up millions of people who find it hard to cater to their basic everyday needs, join us to put a smile on someone's face forever today by giving whether material or financial possession, and you'll experience the joy that comes with making another person smile.

AdnegsCare Foundation is a foundation that was created to help those who find it hard to cater for themselves. Whether it be through providing food, clothing, or shelter, we aim to assist those in need and help them get back on their feet. We believe that everyone should be given a chance to succeed, no matter their circumstances, and with the help of the community we can make this happen. At AdnegsCare Foundation, we are committed to helping those in need by bridging the gap between those who struggle to cater for themselves and those who can.
